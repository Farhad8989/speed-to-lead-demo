# SEO Services — Sales Knowledge Base

## What We Offer
Full-service SEO for small and mid-sized businesses. We handle everything: technical audits, on-page optimisation, content strategy, link building, and monthly reporting. We do NOT do paid ads (that's a separate Digital Marketing engagement).

## Packages & Pricing
| Package     | Monthly Retainer | Best For |
|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| Starter     | $800 – $1,200   | Local businesses, new websites, 1–5 target keywords |
| Growth      | $1,500 – $2,500 | Regional businesses, competitive niches, blog + link building |
| Authority   | $3,000 – $6,000 | E-commerce, national brands, 50+ keywords, full content calendar |

- Minimum engagement: 3 months (SEO takes time — avoid clients expecting week-1 rankings)
- Setup fee: $500 one-time (waived on Authority package)
- Reporting: monthly rankings + traffic report, quarterly strategy review

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Has an existing website (we can recommend web dev if they don't)
- Budget $1,000+/month — below $800 we can't do meaningful work
- Understands SEO is a 3–6 month play, not instant
- Has a clear geographic or niche target (e.g. "plumbers in Melbourne" or "SaaS HR tools")
- Decision maker is on the call / conversation (not "I need to check with my boss")

## Disqualifiers — Politely Decline If:
- Budget under $600/month
- Wants guaranteed #1 rankings (red flag — no ethical agency promises this)
- Needs results within 4 weeks
- Completely new business with no website or brand yet (refer to Web Dev first)
- In a heavily regulated industry (gambling, crypto, adult) — outside our scope

## Key Qualifying Questions to Ask
1. What's the main goal — more local foot traffic, national online sales, or leads from Google?
2. Do you have an existing website? How old is it?
3. Have you done any SEO before? If so, what happened?
4. What's your monthly budget range for SEO?
5. How soon are you looking to get started, and what does success look like in 6 months?

## Scoring Guide
- **HOT**: Budget $1,000+, has a website, understands timeline, clear goal, wants to start within 30 days
- **WARM**: Interested, budget unclear or slightly low ($600–$999), timeline vague (2–3 months), needs more education
- **COLD**: Budget under $600, expects instant results, no website, or just researching with no intent to buy

## Common Objections & Responses
- *"Can you guarantee rankings?"* → "No ethical SEO agency can — Google's algorithm changes constantly. What we guarantee is best-practice work, transparent reporting, and a clear process. Our clients typically see meaningful movement in 3–4 months."
- *"It's too expensive"* → "SEO compounds over time — unlike ads, rankings you build keep working. The Starter package at $800/month often pays for itself with 2–3 new clients per month."
- *"We tried SEO before and it didn't work"* → "That's common. Most failures come from keyword stuffing, spammy links, or no content strategy. Ask them what was done — then differentiate."
