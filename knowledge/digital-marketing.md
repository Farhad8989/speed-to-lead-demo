# Digital Marketing — Sales Knowledge Base

## What We Offer
Paid advertising management across Google Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, and LinkedIn. We also run email marketing campaigns and social media management. We do NOT do organic SEO (separate service) or influencer management.

## Packages & Pricing
| Package         | Management Fee     | Min. Ad Spend | Best For |
|-----------------|--------------------|---------------|----------|
| Starter Ads     | $800/month         | $1,000/month  | Local businesses, single platform (Google or Meta) |
| Growth          | $1,500/month       | $3,000/month  | Multi-platform, A/B testing, retargeting |
| Scale           | $3,000+/month      | $8,000+/month | E-commerce, national campaigns, full funnel |
| Email Marketing | $600–$1,200/month  | N/A           | List nurturing, drip campaigns, Klaviyo/Mailchimp |

- Management fee is separate from ad spend (ad spend goes directly to platforms)
- Minimum 2-month engagement to gather data and optimise
- Weekly performance reports, monthly strategy calls
- We do NOT guarantee ROAS — we guarantee a rigorous testing and optimisation process

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Has a product/service with a proven offer (we amplify what works, we don't fix broken offers)
- Willing to invest in both management fee AND ad spend
- Has a functional landing page or website to send traffic to
- Understands paid ads need 4–6 weeks of data before meaningful optimisation
- Open to testing and iteration (not "set and forget")

## Disqualifiers — Politely Decline If:
- Ad spend budget under $500/month (not enough data to optimise)
- Expects immediate 10x ROAS from week one
- Has no landing page or website (refer to web dev first)
- Wants to run ads but hasn't defined their target audience or offer
- Business model is unclear or product hasn't sold yet organically

## Key Qualifying Questions to Ask
1. Which platform are you interested in — Google, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, or multiple?
2. Have you run paid ads before? If so, what worked or didn't?
3. What's your monthly ad spend budget (separate from our management fee)?
4. What's the goal — leads, online sales, app installs, brand awareness?
5. Do you have a landing page or website ready to receive paid traffic?

## Scoring Guide
- **HOT**: Clear goal, ad spend $2,000+/month, has a landing page, has run ads before (or is very clear on offer), wants to start within 2–4 weeks
- **WARM**: Interested, ad spend unclear or $500–$2,000, no landing page yet, needs education on the process
- **COLD**: Ad spend under $500, expects guaranteed results, no product/offer defined, or just "seeing what's out there"

## Common Objections & Responses
- *"We tried Facebook ads and lost money"* → "That's the most common story. Usually it's a targeting, creative, or landing page problem — not the platform. We audit what was done before recommending a strategy."
- *"Can you guarantee results?"* → "We can't guarantee a specific ROAS — no honest agency can. We guarantee a structured testing process, transparent reporting, and ongoing optimisation. Most clients see meaningful improvement by month 2–3."
- *"Why do I pay a management fee AND ad spend?"* → "Ad spend goes directly to Google/Meta — we never touch it. The management fee is for our time building, monitoring, and optimising your campaigns."
