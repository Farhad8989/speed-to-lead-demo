## Ideal Customer Profile (General)
- Has a business problem they want solved — not just browsing
- Has a realistic budget for professional services
- Is a decision maker or has direct access to one
- Has reasonable timeline expectations

## Disqualifiers
- No budget ("I just want to see what it costs" with zero intent)
- Wants guaranteed outcomes with no flexibility
- Not a decision maker and unable to loop in the right person

## Key Qualifying Questions
1. What's the main challenge or goal that brought you here today?
2. Have you worked with an agency before? What was that experience like?
3. What's your timeline, and is there a specific event or deadline driving it?
4. What budget range are you working with?
5. Who else is involved in making this decision?

## Scoring Guide
- **HOT**: Clear problem, budget confirmed, decision maker, wants to start within 30 days
- **WARM**: Interested but vague on budget, timeline, or scope — needs a discovery call
- **COLD**: No clear problem, no budget, just exploring, or not a decision maker

## Objection Responses
- *"I'm just getting quotes"* → "That makes sense — we're happy to give you a clear picture. To give you an accurate quote, I just need to understand your goal and timeline a bit better."
- *"I need to think about it"* → "Of course. What would help you make a decision — more information about our process, or a quick call with one of our specialists?"
