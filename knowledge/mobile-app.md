# Mobile App Development — Sales Knowledge Base

## What We Offer
Native and cross-platform mobile apps using React Native (iOS + Android from one codebase) or Swift/Kotlin for native-only builds. We handle UX/UI design, development, App Store / Play Store submission, and optional post-launch support retainers.

## Packages & Pricing
| Type                    | Price Range         | Timeline    | Best For |
|-------------------------|---------------------|-------------|----------|
| MVP (React Native)      | $15,000 – $30,000   | 8–12 weeks  | Startups validating an idea, simple 3–5 screen apps |
| Full Product            | $35,000 – $80,000   | 4–6 months  | Feature-rich apps with auth, payments, push notifications, backend |
| Enterprise / Complex    | $80,000+            | 6–12 months | Offline-first, real-time data, complex integrations, large teams |

- Design (UX/UI) included in all packages
- Backend API development quoted separately if needed
- App Store / Play Store submission included
- Post-launch support retainer: $1,500–$3,000/month
- We do NOT build gambling, adult content, or MLM apps

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Has validated the idea (even just wireframes or user interviews — not pure "I have an idea")
- Budget $15,000+ and understands software is not a one-time cost
- Willing to start with an MVP rather than building everything at once
- Has a product owner / someone who can make decisions quickly
- Understands app store review timelines (Apple: 1–3 days, Google: hours to days)

## Disqualifiers — Politely Decline If:
- Budget under $10,000 for anything beyond a very basic prototype
- Describes a feature set that would take 6+ months but wants it in 6 weeks
- "I just want something like Uber/Airbnb but for X" with a $20k budget
- Has been through 2+ previous agencies without a working product (usually a scope/communication problem — needs careful vetting)
- Wants to own all IP but won't sign a proper contract

## Key Qualifying Questions to Ask
1. What platform — iOS only, Android only, or both?
2. What's the core problem your app solves, and who's the target user?
3. Have you validated this idea — any mockups, competitor research, or early users?
4. What's your budget for the build, and do you have a runway for post-launch maintenance?
5. What's the timeline — is there a specific launch date or event driving this?

## Scoring Guide
- **HOT**: Budget $20,000+, validated idea, clear scope, decision maker engaged, wants to start within 30 days
- **WARM**: Interested, budget $10,000–$20,000 or vague, idea not fully validated, timeline flexible
- **COLD**: Budget under $10,000, "just an idea" with no validation, unrealistic scope/timeline expectations

## Common Objections & Responses
- *"Why is it so expensive?"* → "A mobile app involves design, iOS + Android development, backend, testing, and store submission. Unlike a website, a broken app gets 1-star reviews publicly. Quality has a floor."
- *"Can't you just copy [competitor app]?"* → "We can use it as a reference, but copying is both legally risky and technically harder than it sounds. Let's define what features actually matter for your users."
- *"I just need the MVP for now"* → "Perfect — that's the right approach. Let's define the smallest version that proves your core value proposition."
