# Business Consulting — Sales Knowledge Base

## What We Offer
Strategic and operational consulting for SMBs. We specialise in digital transformation, growth strategy, operations improvement, and technology selection. Engagements are structured as either fixed-scope projects or ongoing advisory retainers. We do NOT do accounting, legal, or HR compliance work.

## Packages & Pricing
| Engagement Type       | Price Range           | Duration     | Best For |
|-----------------------|-----------------------|--------------|----------|
| Strategy Workshop     | $2,500 – $5,000       | 1–2 days     | Businesses needing clarity on direction, priorities, or a specific problem |
| Project Engagement    | $8,000 – $30,000      | 4–12 weeks   | Defined problem: new market entry, process redesign, tech stack selection |
| Advisory Retainer     | $3,000 – $8,000/month | Ongoing      | Fractional CMO/COO support, ongoing strategic guidance |
| Digital Transformation| $20,000 – $80,000     | 3–9 months   | Full operational overhaul, systems integration, team enablement |

- All engagements start with a paid Discovery Session ($500–$1,500) unless scope is clearly defined
- We work with businesses $1M+ annual revenue (smaller businesses usually need execution, not strategy)
- We don't do implementation work ourselves — we scope it and manage vendors

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Business owner or C-suite — not a middle manager fishing for ideas
- Revenue $1M–$50M (sweet spot where strategy has leverage but they still move fast)
- Has a specific, felt pain: "we're losing clients", "our ops are a mess", "we need to scale but don't know how"
- Open to outside perspective and willing to act on recommendations
- Budget for both consulting AND implementation (we advise, they execute or hire vendors)

## Disqualifiers — Politely Decline If:
- Pre-revenue startups (too early for strategy — they need to do things, not plan things)
- Wants free advice disguised as a "quick chat"
- Not a decision maker (manager, employee fishing for ammunition against their boss)
- Wants us to implement as well as advise (conflict of interest — refer to specialists)
- Has engaged 3+ consultants with no action taken (not ready to change)

## Key Qualifying Questions to Ask
1. What's the specific problem or goal driving this — what does success look like in 6 months?
2. What's your current annual revenue, and what's the growth target?
3. Who else is involved in this decision — is this your call to make?
4. Have you worked with consultants before? What happened?
5. What's your budget range, and is there urgency around solving this?

## Scoring Guide
- **HOT**: Decision maker, clear pain point, revenue $1M+, budget $5,000+, wants to start within 4 weeks, previous consultant experience (knows the value)
- **WARM**: Interested, budget or urgency unclear, may need education on consulting ROI, or problem not fully articulated yet
- **COLD**: Pre-revenue, not a decision maker, wants free advice, or no clear problem defined

## Common Objections & Responses
- *"What makes you different from other consultants?"* → "We focus on businesses your size — not enterprise playbooks applied to SMBs. We give you a clear action plan, not a 200-page deck."
- *"Can't I just figure this out myself?"* → "You can — most founders do. The question is whether the cost of figuring it out (time, mistakes) exceeds the cost of getting it right faster. Usually it does."
- *"How do I know it'll work?"* → "We start with a Discovery Session — you'll get value from day one. If it's not a fit after that, you won't be locked in."
